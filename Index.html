Mike Rowe

About

I'm Mike Rowe, a designer born and raised in Australia and currently living in Melbourne. Working at the intersection of product design and brand identity, I've had the privilege of influencing products used and loved by millions. These include Red Bull, NASA, BMW, Google, Wacom, Sony, Toyota, Ralph Lauren, Bwin and more. I'm the co-founder of Semplice, a portfolio tool for designers. Before, I worked as lead product designer & art director at Spotify while serving on the AIGA board of directors in New York.
